## 🗣️ Phrases & Sentences

| Japanese | Romaji | English |
|----------|--------|---------|
| ごはんをください。 | gohan o kudasai | Rice, please. |

---

## 💡 Notes

- “～をください” = “Please give me ~”  
- Particles like "を" mark the object (what you're ordering)
